features:
  - title: 🎬 Subtitle-Based Lessons
    details: Works with TV series and any subtitled video. Each subtitle line becomes a learning unit synced to video and audio—the material comes from content you already want to watch.
  - title: 🔤 Sentence to Words
    details: Split subtitle lines into words and ask “Do you know this?” Known words pass through; unknown words join your personal vocabulary instead of drilling random word lists.
  - title: 🃏 Smart Flashcards
    details: Unknown words become spaced-repetition cards. The app schedules reviews at 1, 3, 7, and 30 days so vocabulary actually sticks.
  - title: 🔊 Sentence Playback on Review
    details: When reviewing a word, play the exact sentence and clip from when you first learned it. Words stay tied to context and emotion—not isolated entries.
  - title: 🧠 Active Recall
    details: Recall first, reveal second—not passive subtitle reading. Every “know / don’t know” decision is active retrieval, far more effective than binge-watching alone.
  - title: 🛡️ Local-First & Privacy
    details: Desktop architecture processes video and subtitles on your machine. Progress and word lists stay local—no need to upload your viewing habits to the cloud.
